  1. E-Commerce Act of 2000 (Republic Act No. 8792)

    1. The E-Commerce Act serves as the foundation for online transactions and digital commerce in the Philippines. It provides the legal framework for:
      1. Electronic Documents & Signatures: The Act gives legal recognition to electronic transactions, contracts, and documents.
      2. Consumer Protection: Ensures that consumers are protected when engaging in online transactions, including clear disclosures of product information, pricing, and transaction terms.
      3. Cybersecurity: Requires businesses to adopt measures to secure online transactions and protect the data of users.

  2. Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173)

    1. The Data Privacy Act ensures the protection of personal data collected during ecommerce transactions. Saints Online Shop is committed to the confidentiality and security of user data, including:
      1. Personal Data Protection: We implement measures to protect personal information such as names, addresses, contact details, and payment data.
      2. Data Collection and Consent: We obtain consent before collecting any personal data and provide users with the right to access, correct, or delete their information.
      3. Security Standards: We comply with industry-standard security practices to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse of personal data.

  3. Consumer Protection Act (Republic Act No. 7394)

    1. The Consumer Protection Act safeguards the rights of consumers in the Philippines. At Saints Online Shop, we are committed to upholding the following:
      1. Product Transparency: We provide clear and accurate information about the products, including descriptions, prices, availability, and terms of sale.
      2. Fair Advertising: We adhere to the law by ensuring that all promotional materials and advertisements on our website are truthful and not misleading.
      3. Right to Return: We comply with the rights of consumers to return or exchange defective products in accordance with our Return and Exchange Policy.

  4. Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10175)

    1. The Cybercrime Prevention Act addresses various online crimes, including fraud, hacking, and identity theft. Saints Online Shop complies with this Act by:
      1. Preventing Fraudulent Activities: We actively monitor for any fraudulent activity, including phishing scams, fraudulent transactions, and unauthorized access attempts.
